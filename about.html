Our Story

About Think India

The story of India's most ambitious student-led movement for national consciousness.

The National Movement

Born from Conviction

Born in 2006 in the corridors of IISc and IIM Bangalore, Think India emerged from a simple, powerful conviction: India's students must not be passive observers of their nation's story. They must be its authors.

"The youth of India are not a statistic. They are the strategy."

Today, Think India is present across 150+ premier universities — IITs, NITs, IIMs, central universities — building India's most robust network of intellectually engaged, nationally conscious young citizens.

IISc Founded IIM Bangalore 150+ Institutes Nation First
NIT Patna Chapter

On the Banks of the Ganga

Founded in 2023 on the banks of the Ganga in the historic heartland of Bihar, the NIT Patna chapter carries forward a legacy of intellectual courage. We are students who refuse to separate their engineering education from their national responsibility.

"We don't just study India's problems. We train ourselves to solve them."

From policy drafting competitions to grassroots outreach, every Think India NIT Patna activity is designed to produce citizens who are as capable in the real world as they are in the classroom.

Our Journey

The Timeline

2006

Think India Founded

Established at IISc and IIM Bangalore by a group of visionary students who believed in the power of informed youth.

2010

Expanding Across IITs

Think India chapters established across major IITs and IIMs, creating a nationwide network of student thinkers.

2018

150+ Institutes

Think India grew to become India's largest student-led policy and leadership forum, spanning over 150 premier institutions.

2023

NIT Patna Chapter Born

The NIT Patna chapter was inaugurated, bringing Think India's vision to Bihar's premier engineering institution.

2026

Youth Jam & Beyond

Hosting major events like Youth Jam 2026, National Debate Championship, and Policy Drafting Challenge — the movement grows stronger.
